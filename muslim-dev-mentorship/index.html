About the Program

This is a free mentorship initiative by Md. Saif Zaman (a software engineer) to help Muslim developers grow in their careers while staying rooted in Islamic values.

As Muslims in tech, we have a unique opportunity to combine our professional excellence with our deen. This mentorship is my sadaqah jariyah — ongoing charity through knowledge sharing.
